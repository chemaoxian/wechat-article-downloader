## English Version

### 1. Purpose

This document outlines the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) copyright complaint process for the WeChat Article Downloader project. We respect intellectual property rights and are committed to responding to valid copyright infringement notices.

### 2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that this project or content downloaded using this tool infringes upon your copyright, please submit a DMCA notice to:

**Contact Information:**
- Email: 443224841@qq.com
- Subject Line: "DMCA Copyright Infringement Notice"

### 3. Required Information

Your DMCA notice must include the following information:

1. **Identification of the copyrighted work** that you claim has been infringed
2. **Identification of the infringing material** - Please provide specific URLs or code locations
3. **Your contact information** - Name, address, telephone number, and email
4. **Statement of good faith** - A statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner
5. **Statement of accuracy** - A statement that the information in the notification is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
6. **Physical or electronic signature** of the copyright owner or authorized agent

### 4. DMCA Notice Template

```
DMCA Copyright Infringement Notice

To: The Copyright Agent
Project: WeChat Article Downloader

1. Identification of copyrighted work:
   [Describe the copyrighted work you own]

2. Identification of infringing material:
   [Provide specific URLs, repository links, or code locations]

3. Contact Information:
   Name: [Your full name]
   Address: [Your mailing address]
   Phone: [Your phone number]
   Email: [Your email address]

4. Good Faith Statement:
   I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

5. Accuracy Statement:
   I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

6. Signature:
   [Your physical or electronic signature]
   Date: [Date]
```

### 5. Response Process

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will:

1. **Acknowledge receipt** within 5 business days
2. **Review the notice** to ensure it contains all required information
3. **Take appropriate action**, which may include:
   - Removing infringing content
   - Disabling access to the material
   - Contacting the parties involved
4. **Notify affected parties** of any actions taken

### 6. Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was wrongfully removed, you may submit a counter-notice with the same contact information and similar required statements.

### 7. Important Notice for Content Creators

**This tool is designed for personal, educational, and research use only.**

- WeChat Article Downloader does NOT store, distribute, or republish any content
- All downloaded content is stored locally on the user's machine
- Users are responsible for compliance with applicable copyright laws
- We encourage users to:
  - Obtain permission from copyright holders before redistributing content
  - Use downloaded content for personal reference only
  - Respect the terms of service of WeChat and content creators

### 8. Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly infringe copyright may have their access to the project restricted, though this is primarily relevant for contributors rather than end users.

### 3. 必需信息

您的 DMCA 通知必须包含以下信息：

1. **版权作品识别** - 描述您声称被侵犯的版权作品
2. **侵权材料识别** - 请提供具体的 URL 或代码位置
3. **您的联系信息** - 姓名、地址、电话和邮箱
4. **善意声明** - 声明您有善意信念认为争议使用未经版权所有者的授权
5. **准确性声明** - 声明通知中的信息准确，且在伪证处罚下，您有权代表版权所有者行事
6. **物理或电子签名** - 版权所有者的物理或电子签名

### 5. 响应流程

收到有效的 DMCA 通知后，我们将：

1. **确认收到** - 在 5 个工作日内
2. **审查通知** - 确保包含所有必需信息
3. **采取适当行动** - 可能包括：
   - 删除侵权内容
   - 禁用对材料的访问
   - 联系相关方
4. **通知受影响方** - 告知采取的任何行动

### 6. 反通知

如果您认为您的内容被错误移除，您可以提交反通知，包含相同的联系信息和类似的必需声明。

- 微信文章下载器不存储、分发或再发布任何内容

重复侵犯版权的用户可能会被限制访问本项目，但这主要适用于贡献者而非最终用户。
